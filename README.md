# Scripts and Tools

## En (enphase)
Usage: Em [pattern] [pattern] ....

Examples:

    $ echo "The fox jumps over the lazy dog" | em lazy

The fox jumps over the **lazy** dog

    $ echo "The fox jumps over the lazy dog" | em lazy T.. "[fd]o[xg]"

**The** **fox** jumps over the **lazy** **dog**
